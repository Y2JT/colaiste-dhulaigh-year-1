The History of Dublin's Music

The music scene in Dublin is lively and vibrant. Dublin prides itself on being a musicial city and has produced such famous musical acts as U2, The Frames and The Dubliners. There are many live music venues in Dublin where you can listen to a range of bands from heavy metal to traditional.
Traditional music is what a lot of people want to hear when they visit Dublin, and there is ample opportunity. The most common place to hear live traditonal music is in a pub, but you should also check any of the regular listings in the newspaper and our events listed below.

The Hot House Band

The Hot House Band are a full 17 piece big band comprised of some of the finest jazz, traditional and profssional musicians in Ireland, so be sure to bring your dancing shoes. Led by Dublin saxophonist and clarinettist Mark Wilde, they are complemented by a choice of leading Irish vocalists at every performace. The band play a regular gig every Monday night (except bank holiday Mondays) at the Mercantile on Dublin's Dame Street where they routinely enthral their audience with their high energy and wide ranging repertoire.
