The History of Cork's Music

In Cork, music tradition was first documented in the sixth century breviary lessons of St. Finbarr, whose monastery had a library. Cork grew to be a music centre of merit. By the start of the nineteenth century, there were nine music shops and eleven firms making musical instruments. Music, once the amusement and preserve of the rich with their private collections, was now being taken up by the middle classes. The establishment of the Opera House in 1855 made musical culture accessible to all and gave focus to creative leisure. The tradition, stories, and anecdotes from the "Gods" (upper balconies) are part of the Cork folklore. Drama, music, and opera have also been a source of pride for Cork critics at all levels of society.

West Cork Chamber Music Festival

26 June - 04 July 2015
The West Cork Chamber Music Festival celebrates it's 20th anniversay by programming some of the great landmarks in the chamber music repertoire. Among the works included will be Schubert's Trout, Brahm's Piano Quintet, Beethoven's Archduke Trio, Mendelssohn's Octet, Mozart's Clarinet Quintet and Bach's Violin Concertos. Lesser known works, for which the festival is famous, will also find their place in the nine day programme. Among the world-class musicians appearing will be Alina Ibragimova, Viviane Hagner, Brett Dean, Barry Douglas, Natalie Clein, Lilli Maijala, Liana Gourdjia, Dmitry Sitkovetsky, Alexander Melnikov, Peter Laul, Philippe Bernold, Peter Whelan, Cremona Quartet, Vanbrugh Quartet, Borodin Quartet, Signum Quartet and Benyounes Quartet. Sopranos Claire Booth and Maria Keohane join tenor James Gilchrist.

G'wan

Born in early Spring 2013 out of the trad session scene in Cork, Ireland, G'wan gathers four musicians from across different seas.All members of the band are Cork city dwellers, and share the same passion for traditional Irish music. The quartet brilliantly blends invigorating tunes from Celtic nations with their own compositions.The result is rich, fresh and dynamic; contemporary traditional music bursting with vidid harmonies and zealous rhythms.
