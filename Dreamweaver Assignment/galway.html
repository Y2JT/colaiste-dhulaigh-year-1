Galway Ramblers

The Galway Ramblers began to meet in music establishments of Galway, like the Crane Bar and Cooke's Thatch Bar. As the band was formed from the sessions of Galway, they decided to try and recreate the feeling of an authentic Irish Traditional Session for their audience.
