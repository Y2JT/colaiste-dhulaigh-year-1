Traditional Irish Music

Traditional Irish music was once the most popular music in Ireland. During the 20th century, new styles of music became more popular.

Some of the earliest form of Irish music is designed for the harp. This type of Irish music is from the 16th century, however, a lot of Irish music was created in the last three hundred years. Irish music and songs were not usually written down, instead, adults taught children how to play the music by ear. Learning the music this way is called the oral tradition. Edward Bunting was the first person to write down harp music, this was during the Belfast Harper's Meeting in 1792.

A lot of traditional Irish music comes from European music. Did you know the dance tune of the reel came from Scotland? Other European music, like the polkas and waltzes, became part of the traditional Irish music style.

Today, Irish music is very popular around the world.
